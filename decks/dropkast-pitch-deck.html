Unit Economics · 11 / 15
Unit Economics

Break-even at 80 artists.

80
Paying Artists × $15/mo = $1,200 MRR
That's where Phase 2 monthly burn meets recurring revenue. Below 80 we run at a controlled loss. Above 80 every paying artist is gross margin into eng + product.
500 artists × 50% conv. × $15 = $3,750 MRR
Phase 2 monthly cost ≈ $1,200
Net contribution: $2,550/mo
